Top 3 Movies of All Time According to Dan

The only movies I will watch more than once.

Office Space

Just so relatable for someone who works in a similar environment. Never gets old.

The Usual Suspects

The characters make this one. Benicio Del Toro especially. He is one of my all-time favorite actors. Has lost some of its luster, given the Kevin Spacey controversy, but still great.

Christmas Vacation

Doesn't get any more classic than this. An annual watch for the entire family.
